# Risks and Mitigations

## AI Garbage Output
- Mitigation: strong output validation and allowlist

## Data Loss
- Mitigation: dry-run default, undo log, and atomic operations

## Performance on Large Folders
- Mitigation: batching, caching, and progress updates

## Privacy Concerns
- Mitigation: clear consent notice and file-type gating

## Rename Collisions
- Mitigation: deterministic conflict resolution and skip rules
